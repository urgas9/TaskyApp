How to use it

We want you to run the app when you are located in an office setting - writing an email, having a meeting, programming, etc.

It is really easy, just before you start one of those tasks please follow the steps below:

  • Open TaskyApp
  • On the first screen choose task difficulty
  • Select time after which you will be performing the task
  • Click "Start sensing" button
  • The app will sense for around 10 seconds and inform you when it is done.

That's it! You can check some data of your activity by clicking "Check statistics" button.

alternative

Automatic task detection

TaskyApp tends to be smart. It tries to detect phone's context switch and starts sensing on its own, only during office hours you provide!

Detected tasks will be presented to you in a list. You choose appropriate task and label it with difficulty scale.

  • Open TaskyApp
  • On the first screen click "Label tasks"
  • Select appropriate task in the list (or on the map)
  • Label it and click "OK"

If you are not sure about the task then leave it or click on "Discard", rather than labelling it.

What happens to my data?

Don't worry! Your data is anonymised and sent to a server located at Faculty of Computer and Information science in Ljubljana.

You can check some of the data by clicking "Statistics" on the first screen. Get your personalized work tasks' stats including:

  • Leaderboard (how many labeled tasks you have provided compared to others)
  • Weekly breakdown of task difficulty
  • Locations associated with different task difficulty
  • A comparison with the other users - are you having it harder than the others?
